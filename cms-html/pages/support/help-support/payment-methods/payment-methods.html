Payment Methods

MyYes App

Manage your all your account needs with ease through our self-care MyYes app.

  • Step 1

    Download the MyYes app

  • Step 2

    Login to your account

  • Step 3

    Select the 'Account' tab

  • Step 4

    Tap on 'Pay' to make bill payment

Scan the QR code to begin

JomPAY/Online Banking

Pay your bills anytime, anywhere at your convenience.

  • Step 1

    Log in to your preferred bank or select JomPAY.

  • Step 2

    Enter Yes JomPAY Biller Code: 22376 & Ref-1 (Billing Account) to confirm payment.

Yes Express Payment Channel

Enjoy quick, easy and secure online bill payment through our designated portal.

Step 1
Visit https://selfcare.yes.my /myselfcare/doExpressPay.do
Step 2

Verify your phone number  

Step 3

Choose 'Pay bills'.

Step 4

Make payment.

Auto Billing/Direct Debit

Enjoy 2% rebate or up to RM5 on your monthly bill when you enable automatic payment.

Step 1

Download MyYes app.

Step 2

Tap 'Auto Bill' on homepage.

Step 3

Switch on 'Enable Auto Billing'

Step 4

Press 'Add Card'

Step 5

Fill in the credit/debit card details and proceed with payment.
