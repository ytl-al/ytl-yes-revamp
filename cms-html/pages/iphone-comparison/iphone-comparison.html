You ask, we answer

The Yes Infinite+ Postpaid Service Plans Programme is a device bundle plan that includes a smartphone with a 24 or 36-month contract.

It is offered to individuals residing in Malaysia and who are 18 years of age and above. Eligible subscribers who successfully pass the credit check process will enjoy RM0 upfront. All other eligible subscribers will be required to pay a device deposit which will be fully rebated over the contract period.

The Infinite Cashback plan provides customers with the freedom and flexibility to own an iPhone without a contract, while still earning cashback monthly for up to 35 months, provided you remain with Yes. Since this is not a contract plan, there are no penalties or early termination charges if you choose to cancel. For more information on the services and plan offerings, please visit www.yes.my/iPhone.

The cashback is as good as cash! It will be credited to your MyYes app wallet. You can use it to:

  • (a) Pay Postpaid bills
  • (b) Purchase prepaid reloads
  • (c) Purchase data add-ons
  • (d) Transfer directly to your bank account
  • (e) Save up to purchase the next iPhone!
