What is eSIM?

eSIM stands for “embedded” SIM, the latest in telecommunications technology widely used in countries such as Japan, South Korea and the USA. This technology uses a virtual SIM card that is built into your device, embracing the convenience and flexibility of eSIM technology.

More about Yes eSIM

Yes eSIM is available via Yes Online Store (YOS), MyYes App, Yes Stores and Yes Exclusive Stores.

There are no charges on Yes eSIM for newly registered and new Port in (MNP) customer. For Yes eSIM replacement, usual SIM replacement charges will apply.

Yes, you may sign up to any Yes Prepaid and Postpaid mobile plans using a Yes eSIM as long as your device is eSIM supported. Yes eSIM does not support any broadband devices.
