What is eSIM?

eSIM stands for “embedded” SIM, the latest in telecommunications technology widely used in countries such as Japan, South Korea and the USA. This technology uses a virtual SIM card that is built into your device, embracing the convenience and flexibility of eSIM technology.

More about Yes eSIM

Yes eSIM is available via Yes Online Store (YOS), MyYes App, Yes Stores and Yes Authorised Dealers.

There are no charges on Yes eSIM for newly registered and new Port in (MNP) customer. For Yes eSIM replacement, usual SIM replacement charges will apply.

Yes, you may sign up to any Yes Prepaid and Postpaid mobile plans using a Yes eSIM as long as your device is eSIM supported. Yes eSIM does not support any broadband devices.

In order to have the best experience on Yes 5G network on your iPhone XS or later models, you will need to update to the latest carrier settings version 56.1 and iOS 17.1 or later versions.

On your iPhone, please follow the simple steps below:
(i) Go to Settings > General > Software Update > Update to the latest iOS version
(ii) Go to Settings > General > About > If prompted, follow the steps to update to the latest carrier settings.

For older iPhones, please enable VoLTE with the following steps:
(i) Go to Settings > Mobile Data > Voice & Data > Turn On VoLTE

Note: Please back up your device using iCloud or your computer
