Step 2
Download the MyYes app, open the application and click on “New User”.

Step 3

Step 4
Scan the barcode found on the back of the SIM jacket or manually input the SIM serial number.

Step 5
Accept the assigned number and click “Next”.

Step 6
Complete your Primary SIM Activation by following the ID and face recognition verification process (eKYC).

Step 7
Proceed with number selection for your supplementary mobile lines, “Book” the chosen number and “Continue” to complete SIM activation. *You may insert the SIM cards for supplementary lines into your supported mobile devices.

Step 8
A confirmation message of your activation will be sent upon completion of verification process (eKYC).
