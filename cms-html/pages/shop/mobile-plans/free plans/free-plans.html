Frequently Asked Questions

For the NUTP Free SIM, you need to be a member registered under the National Union Of The Teaching Profession Malaysia ("NUTP"). If you believe you qualify for registration, please visit http://www.nutp.org.my/ for member registration. In addition, you do not have more than five prepaid service accounts with Yes.

For KGKS Free SIM, you need to be a member registered under the KGKS (Kesatuan Guru-Guru Kerajaan Sabah). In addition, you do not have more than five prepaid service accounts with Yes.

For the Yes x FrogAsia Free SIM, YTL Foundation will notify you if you are eligible to participate for this programme. In addition, you must not have more than five prepaid service accounts with Yes.

For New User:

  • Download the MyYes app
  • On the login page, press "New User"
  • Press "Activate SIM" button and follow the self-activation steps on the screen
  • Once your activation is successful, you can now login to your Service account using your phone number and the password sent to your email. You can also login using the TAC method if you cannot find your password.

For Existing User:

  • Login to the MyYes app
  • Go to Quick Access, press "Activate SIM" button and follow the self-activation steps on the screen
  • Once your activation is successful, you can now login to your Service account using your phone number and the password sent to your email. You can also login using the TAC method if you cannot find your password.

For NUTP Free SIM & KGKS Free Sim, your service account will remain active for a period of 60 days.

For Yes x FrogAsia Free SIM, your service account will remain active for a period of 12 months.
