Switch to YES while Keeping Your Number

Switch to YES during your SIM card activation process or anytime after that

Step 1

Get a Plan

Get started with any of our plans available, we have something for everyone. You’ll first recieve your SIM card with a temporary YES number.

Step 2

Activate

Once you receive your SIM, download our MyYes app to activate and verify yourself before switching to your original number.

Learn how to activate

Step 3

Switch

You can switch to your own number during the activation process or anytime after that.

Scroll down to know more

How To: Switch During Activation Process

Step 1

Download the MyYes app, click on ‘New User’ to get started.

Step 2

Head over to ‘Activate SIM’ to insert your information and activate your SIM card.

Step 3

You will receive a confirmation SMS from your existing service provider. Reply to confirm your switch.

How To: Switch Anytime After Activation

Step 1

Download the MyYes app to Keep Your Number even long after your activation.

Step 2

Head over to ‘Quick Access’, and select “Switch to YES” then “Keep Your Number”.

Step 3

You will receive a confirmation SMS from your existing service provider. Reply to confirm your switch.
